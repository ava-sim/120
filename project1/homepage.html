weston estate

ya aunty's favorite boyband
Weston Estate is a North Carolina based boyband consisting of 5 members. The name Weston Estate comes from an affluent neighborhood that member Tanu lived near. Self described as “ya aunty's favorite boyband.” Weston Estate's music has spanned across multiple genres, from the hypnotic lo-fi melodies on “Saturday Nights,” or the slow guitar ballad “Slowly,” to the pop-centric “So Good.” In early 2022, the group released their first project, “Maggie Valley.” Their newest project is called "Superbloom".
