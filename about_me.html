Favorite Restaurant

My favorite restaurant is located in Philadelphia and is called Graffiti Bar. The owner has a few restaurant in the area and they have a lot of yummy small bites such as their edamame dumplings (surprising but very good), dan dan dumpling, and tuna rice crackers. The service is very good and it's close to city hall.


Favorite Food

One of my favorite foods is a Korean food called galbi. It's beef short ribs and marinated in a sauce and then barbecued. I usually eat it alongside rice and kimchi. Sometimes I wrap it in letucce and add a spicy sauce.
